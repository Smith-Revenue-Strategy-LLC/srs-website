Real AI skills, learned in the room. Not another webinar.

AI Collab - Waco is a hands-on AI workshop series for Waco owners, operators, and builders. Small groups, real problems, and a plan you can use Monday morning. We also run private sessions for a single team, built around your own workflows.

What we have done

We ran the first one in June. People left with a plan, not a pile of notes.

The first AI Collab workshop ran June 25 and 26 at 25N Coworking in downtown Waco. Two afternoons, hands-on, with tables grouped by skill level so builders worked with builders and operators worked with operators. Everyone walked out with a clear map of where AI actually helps in their work and a next step to run with.

  • Format Eight hours across two afternoons, with guided working time on real problems.
  • Group Small tables by experience level, with guide support kept close.
  • Focus AI fundamentals, data safeguards, workflow fit, and a next-step plan.
