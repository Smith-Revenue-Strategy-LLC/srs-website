What to expect

Leave with a confident AI direction, not another pile of tool noise.

This workshop is built for people who know AI matters but need a practical way to sort the opportunity, the risk, and the next move. The format pairs short teaching blocks with hands-on work so each participant can apply the material to a real business or role-specific problem.

  • Format Eight hours across two afternoon sessions with guided working time.
  • Support Table groups aligned by experience level, with guide support capped at a 5:1 ratio.
  • Focus AI fundamentals, data safeguards, secure adoption, workflow fit, and next-step planning.
