Decision criteria

A good recommendation should make the tradeoffs visible.

Before a larger project, SRS helps leaders weigh business value, team adoption, security requirements, data readiness, and ongoing support needs.

Business value What changes if the workflow improves, and how that impact should be tracked.
Technical reality Whether existing systems can support the process or need a different operating layer.
Human adoption How the workflow will be documented, governed, and used by the team after launch.
