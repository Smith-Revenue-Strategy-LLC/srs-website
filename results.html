What gets measured

Examples of the business signals SRS works to improve.

Every engagement should connect operational change to visible business value. These are the types of metrics that shape the findings summary and next-step recommendation.

Cycle time How long work sits between handoffs, approvals, systems, or owners.
Manual touches Where people repeat data entry, chase updates, or rebuild the same report.
Revenue capacity How much team time can move back toward selling, serving, and strategic follow-up.

Example scenarios

Common situations where the assessment creates clarity.

sales_handoff

Leads stall between teams

Map the handoff, identify the missing ownership points, and define which notifications, fields, or automations would reduce follow-up delay.

reporting_drag

Leaders rebuild the same reports

Trace the data sources, remove duplicate manual steps, and define the simplest reporting layer leaders can trust.

tool_sprawl

Teams work across too many systems

Decide whether to integrate, simplify, replace, or document the workflow before adding another platform.
