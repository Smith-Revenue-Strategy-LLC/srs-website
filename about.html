The guide behind the work

Rodney Smith

Founder, Smith Revenue Strategy. Revenue consultant, workflow strategist, and operator who has seen both sides of what systems do to people.

I didn't set out to be a workflow consultant.

I've worked inside sales organizations that ran like a well-oiled machine: clear priorities, clean systems, aligned teams. Work moved forward. People had margin. There was energy at the end of the day.

I've also worked inside the opposite.

Layered approvals. Broken handoffs. Constant friction. Everything felt harder than it should be. And what stood out most wasn't just the business impact. It was what that strain did to people. You could feel it follow them home.

That contrast stuck with me.

I became convinced that how a business operates internally doesn't just affect performance. It shapes the lives of the people inside it. Their stress, their time, their presence with family... it's all connected.

That's why I do this work.

I help organizations remove friction, simplify workflows, and build systems that actually support the people using them. Not just so the business can grow, but so the people in it can breathe a little more when they walk through their front door at the end of the day.

Because better systems don't just build better companies. They build better lives.
