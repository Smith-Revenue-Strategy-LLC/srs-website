Engagement path

What happens first?

The process is designed to keep the first decision small, practical, and evidence-based before any larger implementation recommendation is made.

01

Complimentary Connect Call

We start with a 30-minute initial consult to understand your situation and potential fit.

02

Workflow Assessment

If there is a mutual fit, we recommend the right assessment option based on your project scope.

03

Workflow Review

We determine which steps can be automated, eliminated, or simplified with a human in the loop.

04

Findings Summary

You receive a Findings Summary and Value Assessment Presentation so you can choose the clearest path.

Decision criteria

A good recommendation should make the tradeoffs visible.

Before implementation, SRS helps leaders weigh business value, team adoption, security requirements, data readiness, and ongoing support needs.

Business value What changes if the workflow improves, and how that impact should be tracked.
Technical fit Whether existing systems can support the process or need a different operating layer.
Human adoption How the workflow will be documented, governed, and used by the team after launch.
