Privacy Policy

This draft privacy policy is provided as a practical starting point for Smith Revenue Strategy and should be reviewed by legal counsel or a privacy policy provider before publication.

Information We Collect

When you contact Smith Revenue Strategy, we may collect your name, email address, company name, and the business challenge you choose to share. We may also collect basic website analytics such as pages viewed, browser type, and approximate location if analytics tools are added to the site.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, evaluate whether Smith Revenue Strategy may be a fit for your needs, provide requested services, improve our website, and maintain business records.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, manage email, schedule calls, or deliver services. We may also share information when required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No website or transmission method is completely secure, so sensitive business information should be shared only through agreed-upon secure channels.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your submitted information by contacting Smith Revenue Strategy. Some records may be retained when needed for legal, security, or legitimate business purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Smith Revenue Strategy through the contact form or email address listed on the website.
